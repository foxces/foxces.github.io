About

The Proper Atmosphere Is Critical For a Professional To Succeed. Want to upgrade your business to a professional atmosphere, Look no further Fox Chapel Executive Suites are the answer.

Fox Chapel Executive Suites are conveniently located at 634 Alpha Drive in the prestigious Fox Chapel area, RIDC Park, right off of route 28, just minutes from Downtown Pittsburgh.

The offices are appointed with the finest all wood mahogany and leather furniture, etched glass, vaulted ceilings and a state-of-the-art phone system. The building boasts a unique contemporary architecture and an illuminated lobby waterfall.

Suites consist of a single office, with a shared receptionist, executive conference rooms, kitchen, copier, fax and postage services, training facility and on-site computer technician. Parking is free and you will have 24/7 access to the facility.

Our hassle-free service agreements cut out any attorney or legal fees associated with leases and allow for quick startup.

Monthly rent is $800 and up (more expensive office includes private bathroom with shower) and the service agreements range from monthly to three years.
